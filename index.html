ABOUT

Bashiru is a skilled Cloud engineer with experience in network automation, infrastructure and application deployment. With over 6 years experience in IT/Telecommunication, and 2 years experience in cloud technologies, I can deploy and manage infrastructure as well as applications both on premises and in the cloud. Currently working to gain hands-on skills in DevOps using various technologies and tools such as: Kubernetes, Docker, Ansible, Terraform, Web Servers and Databases

I also have vast experience in Telecommunication design and build, managing data centers and access network infrastructure design using coaxial cable and fibre.

I graduated with a Masters degree in Electronic Systems Engineering from the University of Regina, SK, Canada, Bachelor of Engineering in Electrical/Electronic Engineering (graduated with First Class honors) and Diploma in Computer Engineering (graduated with Distinction) from the University of Benin, Nigeria. I attended formal trainings in Cloud and DevOps engineering as well as a robust self-directed learning of tools and technologies to architect, build, automate, deploy and manage applications.
